CODEX17 messengers · pattern blue · biological singularities

The Angels (使徒, shito, "apostles") are biological singularities of unknown origin, each carrying an S² engine and an Absolute Terror Field. Of the 18 prophesied in the Dead Sea Scrolls, two are progenitors (Adam, Lilith) and one is the human race itself (Lilim). The 15 between are described below in chronological order of contact.

03 18TH ANGEL

The 18th and final Angel, according to the Dead Sea Scrolls, is humanity itself — Lilim (リリン), the children of Lilith. Distinct from the other Angels in that they reproduce sexually and lack a true S² engine. The Human Instrumentality Project is, ultimately, a strategy to resolve the existence of this 18th Angel by merging all Lilim into a single being and ending the cycle of contact.

"The Lilim... that's what you call yourselves. The 18th Angel. The last." — Kaworu Nagisa // Eva ep. 24

See INSTRUMENTALITY PROJECT for the SEELE doctrine on resolving the 18th Angel problem.
